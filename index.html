I hold a B.S. in Data Science and Mathematics from Tecnológico de Monterrey (ITESM). I currently collaborate with The Carter Center, building LLM workflows to analyze legal texts on freedom of expression and association.

My background spans both research and industry. I’ve worked on data systems at CEMEX and developed AI pipelines for global NGO regulation data at Concordia University.

Long term, I’m interested in making AI systems accessible, fair, and globally relevant, especially in regions like Latin America.
