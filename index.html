About

```

I currently collaborate with The Carter Center, where I've built LLM-based workflows to analyze and structure laws on freedom of expression. This work contributes to building cross-country datasets for policy research.

During my undergraduate studies, I worked across both industry and research. At CEMEX, I built SQL-based data systems and dashboards for operational metrics. At Concordia University, I worked on automating data extraction for the GRNDS project, which compiles and standardizes global NGO regulation data.

I’m interested in applied LLM systems, especially agent-based RAG systems and evaluation. Long term, I want to build accesible AI systems that support people in policy and climate. I care especially about ensuring Mexico and Latin America have a role in the development of these systems.

```
