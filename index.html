About Me

Hello, I'm Raúl, I hold a B.S. in Data Science and Mathematics from Tecnológico de Monterrey (ITESM). My work focuses on applied AI and machine learning in policy, society, and climate. I'm interested in how humans and AI interact in decision-making. I currently collaborate with The Carter Center. I've built LLM-based workflows to codify laws on freedom of expression. This work contributes to building structured, cross-country datasets for policy research. During my undergraduate studies, I worked across both industry and research, I built SQL data systems and dashboards at CEMEX, and developed AI pipelines for legal data extraction at Concordia University.
